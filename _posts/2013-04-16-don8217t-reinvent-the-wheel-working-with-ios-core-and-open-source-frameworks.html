“We’re different!” This is a mantra many organizations will trot out to justify striking out on their own path with some new technology, design, or process.And sometimes it’s true.But the question they need to ask next is, “Are we so different as to justify two to four times as much work, a delay in getting to market, and increased maintenance costs for the indefinite future?” Nowhere is this more apparent than the app ecosystem of the mobile computing world.
