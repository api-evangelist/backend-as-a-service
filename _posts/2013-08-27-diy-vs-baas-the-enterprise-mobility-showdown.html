Today, we’ve released a free eBook titled “DIY vs.BaaS: The Enterprise Mobility Showdown,” a comparative guide aimed to help enterprises evaluate the best approach for integrating their mobile apps with enterprise systems and data.Mapping out a mobile strategy is the crucial first step for any enterprise looking to mobilize business data. Effective plans zero in on vital details, like how the organization will integrate mobile apps securely and effectively with their existing backend infrastructure.Often this decision comes down to “doing it” oneself or using a cloud backend provider.
