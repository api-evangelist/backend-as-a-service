The International Data Corporation, a market research firm, said in a report released Tuesday that the number of smartphones sold in the world will surpass unit sales of more basic feature phones by the end of 2013.The report predicts that shipments of smartphones will be 32.7 percent higher this year than last year, reaching 958.8 million units.This is up from 722.
