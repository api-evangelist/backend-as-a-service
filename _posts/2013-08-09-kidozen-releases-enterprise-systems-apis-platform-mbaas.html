KidoZen, a leading enterprise mobile middleware provider, announced the release of the KidoZen Enterprise Systems APIs platform which allows developers to seamlessly integrate corporate and enterprise systems into mobile applications.The platform consists of a new group of Enterprise APIs which includes Podio, NetSuite, Salesforce, SAP, SharePoint and many others.KidoZen is an enterprise mobile backend as a service platform (mBaaS) that allows developers to quickly and easily incorporate backend functionality such as databases, storage, security and push notifications into their mobile enterprise applications.The platform consists of four key components: Mobile backend services – Services that enable enterprise mobile application capabilities such as authentication, data storage, push notifications, SMS, email and more.Enterprise mobile app store – Allows enterprises to offer their own mobile applications in an App store environment.
