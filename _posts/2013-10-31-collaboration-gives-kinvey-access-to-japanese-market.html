Today ACCESS CO., a global, Tokyo-based software and services company serving the mobile market, announced it has teamed up with Kinvey to develop sophisticated mobile apps for Japanese businesses.ACCESS is already building “smart device” apps on our platform, and the company will now begin to resell Backend as a Service (BaaS) solutions to enterprises in Japan.  We will provide ACCESS with marketing, sales and customer support resources.Together, we believe this collaboration will open the Japanese market to BaaS in a meaningful way, particularly as trends like bring your own device and platform fragmentation rise in Japan.
