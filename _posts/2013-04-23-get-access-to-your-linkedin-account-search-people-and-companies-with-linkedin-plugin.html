Our new LinkedIn Plug-in allows you to get access to your LinkedIn account, read your profile data, read data from your connection profile, get a list of connections, search people and companies, and read data from a company profile.To add this plug-in to an app, you will just need to follow these steps in Appery.io: From the Appery.io builder, select Create New and then From Plug-in.Click the check box for Linked oAuth and then click on the Import selected plugins button.
