It is officially summer in the northern hemisphere and as always we are happy to bring you our popular Summer School program.Since 2009, our interactive web-based program has helped thousands of architects and developers learn about upcoming technologies and best practices for integrating and deploying enterprise applications.This year for our sixth year of Summer School, we have exciting new classes on IoT to discuss the latest developments and get a deeper understanding of the challenges that lie ahead.I will be kicking off the first session tomorrow, providing an introduction and reference architecture for IoT.This session will be followed by: Device Management for Connected Devices, on July 3, presented by Sinnathamby Shanmugarajah, director of mobile architecture at WSO2.
