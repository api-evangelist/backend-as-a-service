Concrete Software is a 25 person company in Minneapolis, Minnesota with a passion for building and publishing “rock solid” mobile games.Building games for feature phones (using J2ME), BlackBerry, Windows Mobile, and Palm OS since 2003, one of their biggest hits to date is PBA® Bowling Challenge.A freemium bowling game with multiplayer, career mode, more than 10 tournaments, and the real PBA players to play against, the app was built through close work with the Professional Bowlers Association.Concrete Software turned to Parse to add push notifications to their apps.They use notifications to push relevant information to players, such as advertisements for limited sales and important updates with significant bug fixes or additions.
