Today Apple approved our new release: ChattAR 2.0!It is a revised application with a rich functionality and is coolly designed for iOS7.In particular, it allows real time group chatting between people sharing the same interest and has a search mode in Augmented Reality: By tradition - a big thanks to those who participated in the development and production of the application, especially the main developer of the project Igor Alefirenko and Evgen for a great design!
